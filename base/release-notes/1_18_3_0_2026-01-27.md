Hello everyone!


Today, we are releasing two new Creator Packs for Crusader Kings III: _High Medieval Warfare Attire_, created by JedNick and _Holy Buildings_, created by Kefir úr.

On top of that, we're releasing Update 1.18.3 containing bug fixes from the public beta branch we shared before the holidays. A **big** thank you goes out to everyone who helped out with testing and providing feedback.


Check the changelog below for more information!


---


## Update 1.18.3 Changelog

### Bug Fixes

-   Fixed a crash related to generating portrait DNA


-   Fixed a crash related to invalid situations in the situation-window


-   Updated the liberty faction creation trigger for all administrative governments


-   Fixed liberty faction war to always reduce the appropriate law


-   Fixed converting to feudalism as an independent tribal Japanese character sometimes turning you into an Administrative realm


-   Fixed incorrectly displayed requirements for the Found the Empire of Brunei decision


-   Fixed requirements for Farm Estates not allowing construction of level 1 through 4 for cultures with Pastoralists tradition


-   Fixed watermills not unlocking correctly in city holdings for cultures with City Keepers tradition


-   Chinese nobility will no longer use Western trousers


-   Chariots are no longer always stuck in the mud


-   Updated the Withdraw from Governorship decision for Ministers to correctly remove them from the Council


-   Fixed missing treasury payouts for Fertility Effort and Natural Disaster Insurance from Conduct Census Great Project


-   Prevented Ministers from becoming bankrupt from Great Project optional contribution payouts


-   Ensured that the Grand Campaign will complete after a Grand Campaign war is won and there is no title left to conquer
